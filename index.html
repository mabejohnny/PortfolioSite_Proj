Hi, I'm Josh.

Who is Josh?


Self-motivated and always curious to learn, combined with the love of writing clean and elegant code, Josh is a software developer who designs and builds beautiful progams to change the lives of those around him.



Dedicating his life to help others, Josh is a Veteran of the United States Marine Corps, and recipient of the Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal for leadership while deployed to Afghanistan. After leaving the service, Josh traveled all lower 48 states as a long haul truck driver and received the 2015 North American TCA Highway Angel of the Year award for extraordinary heroism. Nowadays, when hes not practicing horsemanship, urban gardening, dancing with wolves or drinking coffee and taking names, hes enjoying the beauty of life with his wife and children.
